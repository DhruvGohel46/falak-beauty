How to Reach Us

Multiple convenient ways to get to our salon

By Car

Take Race Course Road towards City Mall. We're located just 2 minutes from the mall. Ample parking space available.

By Bus

Take any bus heading to Race Course. Get off at City Mall bus stop and walk 200 meters towards our salon.

By Auto/Taxi

Tell the driver "Falak Beauty Saloon near City Mall, Race Course Road". All local drivers know this location.

On Foot

If you're shopping at City Mall, we're just a 3-minute walk away. Look for our pink signboard on Race Course Road.
